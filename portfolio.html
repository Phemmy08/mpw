Aggregate Calculator for Screening Results

The Aggregate Calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to streamline the process of calculating aggregate scores for screening results. It takes multiple inputs such as academic grades, test scores, and weightings, and computes an accurate final score based on predefined criteria. Built to reduce manual errors and save time, this calculator ensures transparency and efficiency in result processing.

Impact:

  • Accuracy: Eliminates human errors in aggregate computations, ensuring precise results.
  • Efficiency: Significantly reduces the time spent on manual calculations, allowing users to focus on decision-making.
  • Accessibility: Provides an easy-to-use interface that is accessible to a wide range of users, including students, educators, and administrators.
  • Customization: Can be adapted to suit different grading systems and scoring standards, making it versatile for diverse applications.

Church Website Development

This project involved designing and developing a modern, responsive website for a church. The website serves as a central hub for church activities, providing essential information such as service schedules, upcoming events, announcements, and a blog section for sharing inspirational content. It also includes features like a contact form, donation integration, and multimedia content (sermons, worship songs, etc.) to engage the congregation and visitors.

Impact:

  • Improved Communication: Ensures that church members and visitors can easily access timely information about services, events, and announcements.
  • Enhanced Community Engagement: Facilitates interaction through features like event sign-ups, online donations, and multimedia content.
  • Increased Reach: A responsive design ensures accessibility across all devices, helping the church connect with a broader audience, including those unable to attend physically.
  • Modern Branding: Elevates the church's digital presence, making it appealing to younger demographics and newcomers.
